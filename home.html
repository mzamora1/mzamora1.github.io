Cractivity 2 Description

Follow the adventures of a decrepit alcoholic in Typing with Dead Pirate Jack as he regails children with (possibly fabricated) tales of his exploits on the high seas. With an amazing 30 seconds of audio and 20 minutes of work in MS Paint, Jack is sure to keep the kiddies begging to put everything entertaining on hold for some overly long typing exercises. Rated E for Ellendig

Test your mental might in this ultimate game of wit and strategy. With each dot tracing, in perfect historical detail, the object which must be represented, this game is a must-have for historians and the feeble-minded everywhere.
